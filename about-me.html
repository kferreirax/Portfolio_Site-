ABOUT ME

Kelsey Ferreira is an artist from New Bedford, MA. She studied Visual Design at a vocational high school, and is continuing her education at UMass Dartmouth as a Graphic Design major. She is consistently involved in her community as volunteers frequently with her sorority, Phi Sigma Sigma. Here, she holds positions such as the Member Development Chair, Risk Chair, and T-Shirt and Insignia Chair.

Along with Graphic Design, Kelsey minors in Business Administrative where she expands her understanding of communication, and studies subjects such as marketing, management, and leadership and teamwork.
